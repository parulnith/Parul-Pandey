Agents-MCP-Hackathon — ReadRight

An AI Reading Coach that helps non-native English speaking students improve reading and pronunciation through personalized stories, guided listening, recording and tailored AI feedback; new stories are generated based on performance.

Demo / post

Gemma 3n Hackathon - ASHA-G

ASHA-G is an offline, privacy-first app that digitizes paper health forms for ASHA workers. Workers snap a photo of a form, the app detects fields and accepts Hindi voice input which is transcribed into Devanagari in real time, and the completed form is exported as a structured CSV for secure storage and integration with health systems.

Demo / writeup

Connected Canvas — Nano Banana / Gemini 2.5 (Flash image)

Transforming screenshots and video-call frames into timeless family portraits: an on-device prototype using a Gemini 2.5 flash image on Nano Banana for image enhancement, background removal, color grading and stylized rendering to create keepsake portraits from casual screenshots.
