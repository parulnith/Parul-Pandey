Agents-MCP-Hackathon — ReadRight#

An AI Reading Coach for students who speak English as a second language. It guides reading, gives feedback, and creates new stories based on progress.

Gemma 3n Hackathon — ASHA-G#

ASHA-G is an offline tool that turns paper health forms into digital records. A worker takes a photo of a form, fields are detected, Hindi speech is turned into text, and the record is saved as a CSV.

Demo / writeup

Connected Canvas — Nano Banana / Gemini 2.5 Flash Image#

A small on-device tool that turns screenshots and call frames into warm family portraits. It cleans the image, adjusts color, and creates a styled picture fit for printing.
